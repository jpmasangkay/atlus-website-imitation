Lost Dimension

Information

In Lost Dimension, a strategy RPG published by ATLUS, an extraterrestrial being called THE END is here to bring about the end of the world. Only a group of eleven psychically gifted warriors - each with unique powers - are even remotely powerful enough to challenge this madman, but it won't take long for players to discover The End is not their only foe.
