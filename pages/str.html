SMT: Strange Journey Redux

Information

A spatial distortion full of demons appeared in Antarctica, threatening to engulf the Earth. The United Nations responded by tasking a team of highly advanced specialists with piercing the anomaly and figuring out how to stop it from the source. Expect new story content, additional endings, a new dungeon to explore, and more in the return of this sci-fi tinged SMT adventure.
