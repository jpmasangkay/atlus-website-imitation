Persona 5

Information

It's a game about the internal and external conflicts of a group of troubled high school students who live dual lives as Phantom Thieves. They have the typically ordinary day-to-day of a Tokyo high schooler but they also undertake fantastical adventures by using otherworldly powers to enter the hearts of people. Their power comes from the Persona, the Jungian concept of the "self;" the game's heroes realize that society forces people to wear masks to protect their inner vulnerabilities, and by literally ripping off their protective mask and confronting their inner selves do the heroes awaken their inner power.
