Shin Megami Tensei 3: Nocturne

Information

A high school student in Tokyo, Japan, encounters a mysterious man who believes a recent park clash is connected to the occult. He arrives at a hospital in Shinjuku, where he encounters a mysterious man and witnesses the catastrophic event, the Conception. The boy wakes up altered and realizes he has been turned into a demon. He embarks on a journey to battle demonic forces and bring order to the chaos, awaiting its rebirth. The world awaits its rebirth, but the boy's identity remains uncertain.
